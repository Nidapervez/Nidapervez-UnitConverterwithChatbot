Welcome to Unit Converter! This tool allows you to easily convert between different units of measurement.

Supported Conversions:
1. Length: Convert between meters, kilometers, miles, feet, inches, etc.
2. Weight: Convert between kilograms, grams, pounds, ounces, etc.
3. Temperature: Convert between Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin.
4. Speed: Convert between km/h, mph, m/s, etc.
5. Time: Convert between seconds, minutes, hours, days, etc.

How to Use:
1. Select the category (Length, Weight, Temperature, etc.).
2. Choose the unit you want to convert from and to.
3. Enter the value.
4. Click on the "Convert" button to get the result.

For accurate results, make sure to enter correct values.
